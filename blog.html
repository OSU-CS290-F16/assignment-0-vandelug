The primary issue that I faced with this assignment was accessing the stak server. I was able to connect to OSU via VPN on my mac without issue but when I attempted to connect to that actual server, it would not recognize my username. After much frustration reading the OSU help pages, I resorted to trail and error. Eventually, I accesed the server using my full email address. Previously, just my ONID name (vandelug) was sufficient to access stak.

Another issue that I faced was the aspect ratio on my photo. I could not get it exactly how I wanted it to be but I believe this is in part due to my inability to take a proper selfie. I feel comfortable manipulating things with the img tag but as I stated on the index page, artistic aspects are not my strong suit.

I already had a working knowledge of HTML, git, github, etc. The thing that I learned from this assignment was the center tag. I had seen this tag before but do not recall ever using it. Normally, I have used CSS to handle such things. The one external resource that I used to reference syntax was w3schools

I read over the Piazza posts and responses to maintain my situational awareness with the course. I did not need to use these as resources per se for this assignment since it contained simple, straight forward tasks. Once I get a better feel of the course expectations, I have every intention of being active on Piazza. Hopefully, I can share the limited knowledge that I have and learn from my peers.
